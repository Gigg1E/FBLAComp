    Help Center

    Getting Started

    1. Create an Account

    Click "Sign Up" in the navigation menu and fill out the registration form. Choose "Regular User" if you just want to browse and review businesses, or "Business Owner" if you want to post deals for your business.

    2. Browse Businesses

    Use the search bar on the homepage or click "Businesses" to browse all local businesses. You can filter by category or city to find what you're looking for.

    3. Read Reviews

    Click on any business to view detailed information, ratings, and reviews from other users.

    Writing Reviews

    1. Log In

    You must be logged in to write a review. If you're not logged in, you'll be redirected to the login page.

    2. Go to a Business Page

    Navigate to the business you want to review and click the "Write a Review" button.

    3. Fill Out the Review Form

    Rate the business from 1 to 5 stars, write a title and detailed review (at least 20 characters), and complete the captcha verification.

    4. Submit

    Click "Submit Review" and your review will be posted immediately. You can only submit one review per business.

    Finding Deals

    1. Homepage Deals

    Active deals are featured on the homepage. Click on any deal to view the business details.

    2. Business Page Deals

    When viewing a business, any active deals will be displayed at the top of the page with discount information and expiration dates.

    For Business Owners

    1. Create a Business Owner Account

    When signing up, select "Business Owner" as your account type.

    2. Post Deals

    After logging in as a business owner, click "Post Deal" in the navigation menu. Fill out the deal form with title, description, discount amount, and validity dates.

    3. Manage Deals

    You can edit or delete your deals from your account page.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I edit my review after submitting it?
    Currently, reviews cannot be edited after submission. You can delete your review and submit a new one if needed.
    How many reviews can I write for one business?
    You can only submit one review per business to ensure fairness and prevent spam.
    Why do I need to complete a captcha?
    The captcha helps prevent spam and automated fake reviews, ensuring all reviews are from real people.
    How do I become a business owner on the site?
    When creating your account, select "Business Owner" as your account type. This will give you access to post deals for your businesses.
    Are the deals verified?
    Deals are posted directly by business owners. We recommend calling the business to confirm the deal before visiting.
    How do I contact support?
    For support inquiries, please email support@localreviews.com with your question or concern.
